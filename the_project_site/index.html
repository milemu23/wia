Step 1: Request

Know someone that could use some art for their hospital room?

All you have to do is send us a request telling us a little about the person that needs some art for a little extra hope!

Step 2: Create

Artwork is created by an artist.

We have a lot of volunteer artists standing by to create a unique one-of-a-kind piece of art.

Step 3: Mail

The beautiful artwork is on it's way to the recipient.

After the artwork is created by one of our artists, it will be packaged up and sent out to the recipient.

Step 4: Display

Artwork is delivered and displayed.

The artwork is delivered to the recipient with everything that is needed to hang right on the wall and bring some cheer to a plain hospital room!
